1984 — Archive RTE footage remake

🎬 15
1984 — Archive RTE footage remake
Caption
In 1984, I won second place in a Ladies' national bodybuilding competition at the Belltable Theatre, Limerick, Ireland. I was not prepared for the Controversy which sparked when the Bishop of Limerick Dr Jeremiah Newman, questioned "if bodybuilding contests were a valid art form or something that rugged sportsmen might regard as being in dubious taste and that women might find offensive." RTE news broadcast the event and asked ladies in the audience whether it was unladylike and unfeminine, and whether they were offended. I loved their radical, smart and savvy support, which in 1984 was brave. You can watch it here, https://www.rte.ie/archives/2019/0806/1067222-limerick-bodybuilding-contest/ #1984 #WomensStrength #Rebellion
